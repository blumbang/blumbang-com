How it works

IT'S SIMPLE.

01
GET A KAOS

Choose a Blumbang kaos. Each one is hand screen printed in Jeto, Klaten — and carries a unique QR code on the inside label. No two are the same.

02
SCAN ANYWHERE

Wear it somewhere new. Scan the QR with your phone camera — no app needed. Your location is recorded: city, country, timestamp. Forever.

03
WATCH IT GROW

Your kaos builds its own map over time. From Klaten to wherever you go — Tokyo, Amsterdam, Mahé. Every scan adds to the story. It never ends.

Born in Jeto, Klaten

EVERY KAOS
HAS A STORY.
YOURS STARTS
HERE.

Bang Arie has been screen printing since 1998. Every kaos that carries a Living Garment QR passes through his hands — ink pressed by someone who has spent twenty-six years doing exactly this.

The QR is small. The idea is not. A garment that records where you take it. Not a tracker. Not an app. Just a quiet record of the places you've been — attached to something you wear.

When you scan it in a new city, you're not just checking in. You're adding a chapter to something that started in a small workshop in the middle of Java.
